Kitchen gadgets by the season

Winter

Electric water boiler - Having a hot water boiler is essential in the winter months when you want a warm or hot cup of water for your morning tea or hot cereal. Designed to hold multiple cups of water it will keep your water nice and toasty until you are ready to dispense! Winter Gadget

Summer

Frozen dessert maker - In the summer you will most likely crave a frozen treat to cool you down. With a frozen dessert maker you can let your imagination run wild! Create your own desserts at home with only the ingredients you wish to include. Summer gadget

Fall

Slow cooker - A slow cooker is great during the fall time when everyone is going back to school and time seems to be running fast. Just place all of your ingredients in your slow cooker, go about the day and come back to a savory meal. Fall Gadget

Spring

Vegetable spiralizer - Who doesn't like going to a farmer’s market in the springtime and gathering ripe veggies? Now you can make your veggies into pasta with the advent of the vegetable spiralizer. It’s now easier to get your recommended requirement of vegetables a day! Spring Gadget
